Refund Policy

Last updated: February 1, 2026

Summary: Due to the nature of digital goods and AI-generated content, all sales are final. We do not offer refunds once content has been generated or exported.

1. Digital Products

WriteMyVeryOwnBook provides digital services including AI-powered content generation and digital book exports. Once you have:

  • Generated content using our AI system
  • Exported or downloaded your book files
  • Accessed premium features after payment

The transaction is considered complete and non-refundable.

2. Why No Refunds?

Our refund policy exists because:

  • Instant Delivery: Digital content is delivered immediately upon generation
  • AI Processing Costs: Each generation consumes AI computing resources that cannot be recovered
  • Content Ownership: You retain full ownership of generated content, which cannot be "returned"

3. Before You Purchase

We encourage you to:

  • Use the free preview features to test the service
  • Review generated abstracts and outlines before paying for export
  • Read our FAQ and documentation to understand the service
  • Contact us with any questions before purchasing

4. Exceptions

We may consider refunds in exceptional circumstances:

  • Technical Failure: If our system fails to generate or deliver content due to a technical error on our end
  • Duplicate Charges: If you were accidentally charged multiple times for the same purchase
  • Unauthorized Transactions: If your payment method was used without authorization

To request an exception review, contact us within 48 hours of purchase with your transaction details.

5. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback with your bank or credit card company after receiving our digital services, we reserve the right to:

  • Suspend your access to the service
  • Contest the chargeback with evidence of service delivery
  • Pursue recovery of the disputed amount

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or believe you qualify for an exception, please contact us at: support@writemyveryownbook.com

Include your email address, transaction ID, and a description of your concern.
