Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 1, 2026

1. Introduction

WriteMyVeryOwnBook ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our AI book writing service at writemyveryownbook.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly:

  • Account Information: Email address and payment information when you make a purchase
  • Content Data: Book ideas, prompts, and generated content you create using our service
  • API Keys: If you use the BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) option, your API key is stored locally in your browser and is never transmitted to our servers

We automatically collect:

  • Device and browser information
  • IP address and general location
  • Usage data and analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our AI book writing service
  • Process payments and deliver purchased content
  • Send service-related communications
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

Your generated content is encrypted and stored securely. For BYOK users, content is processed through your own API key and we do not retain copies of the generated text. We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit and at rest.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Stripe: For secure payment processing
  • OpenRouter/AI Providers: For AI content generation (Pay Upfront plan only)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your generated content
  • Opt out of marketing communications

7. Content Ownership

You retain 100% ownership and copyright of all content generated using our service. We do not claim any rights to your books, stories, or other creative works.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences. We do not use third-party tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: privacy@writemyveryownbook.com
