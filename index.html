We do not store your books or personal data on our servers. All your book content, drafts, and manuscripts are stored locally in your browser's localStorage. Your books never leave your device unless you choose to export them.

We collect minimal information:

Your book content is private and inaccessible to us. We cannot read, access, or review anything you write. AI generation happens via API calls — your content flows directly from your browser to the AI provider and back.

Since all book data stays in your browser's localStorage, you maintain full control. Clear your browser data = books are removed. Export your books regularly to keep backups.

We use Stripe for payments and OpenRouter/AI providers for book generation. Their privacy policies apply to their respective services.
